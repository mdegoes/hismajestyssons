Seven Days of Family Prayer.

VIIDays · one week
5 minRead aloud each
All Resources
  1. I Day One · Lord's Day

    The Church

    Ask for unity, growth, and effectiveness in your congregation and the wider church. Pray for your church leaders to have wisdom and for the church to be a beacon of truth.

    "I will build my church, and the gates of hell shall not prevail against it." Matthew 16:18
  2. II Day Two · Monday

    Against Evil

    Stand firm against spiritual darkness and the challenges of the age, advocating for righteousness and for life. Pray for a godly culture to prevail.

    "Put on the whole armor of God, that ye may be able to stand against the wiles of the devil." Ephesians 6:11
  3. III Day Three · Tuesday

    The Household

    Seek God's blessing, protection, and guidance for your household. Pray for conviction of sin, for spiritual growth, and for every soul under your roof.

    "As for me and my house, we will serve the Lord." Joshua 24:15
  4. IV Day Four · Wednesday

    The Broader Family

    Intercede for extended family — their well-being, their relationships, and their spiritual growth. Ask God to strengthen bonds, heal any rifts, and bring them closer to Him.

    "Believe on the Lord Jesus Christ, and thou shalt be saved, and thy house." Acts 16:31
  5. V Day Five · Thursday

    City, State & Nation

    Petition for wisdom, integrity, and godly decision-making among those in authority. Pray that leaders would prioritize justice and turn to the one true source of life and wisdom — Christ.

    "When the righteous are in authority, the people rejoice." Proverbs 29:2
  6. VI Day Six · Friday

    Praise & Thanksgiving

    Express gratitude and adoration to God for His goodness, love, and faithfulness. Reflect on the blessings in your life and celebrate the ways God has worked in your family and community.

    "Oh give thanks unto the Lord; for he is good: for his mercy endureth for ever." Psalm 107:1
  7. VII Day Seven · Sabbath Eve

    Confession of Sins

    Acknowledge personal and collective shortcomings, seeking God's forgiveness and transformation. Pray for a humble heart and for God to cleanse and renew you and your household.

    "If we confess our sins, he is faithful and just to forgive us our sins, and to cleanse us from all unrighteousness." 1 John 1:9
